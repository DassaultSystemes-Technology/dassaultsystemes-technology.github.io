Enterprise PBR Shading Model

The Dassault Systèmes Enterprise PBR Shading Model (DSPBR) is an easy-to-use, renderer-independent PBR material for a large variety of industries and use-cases, ranging from high-performance VR applications to high-quality GI renderings.
